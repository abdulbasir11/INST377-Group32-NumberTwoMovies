• To benefit from interest-free installments by product, you can select only interest-free installment products and click the [Order] button to order/pay.
  • If you press the "[Full order] button, you will be able to order/pay for all selected items regardless of the shopping basket.
  • However, if you order/pay for the entire product, you will not be able to receive interest-free installment benefits for each product.
  • The interest-free installment plan is displayed in the separate interest-free installment product area in the shopping cart, and the shipping cost is displayed based on the interest-free installment plan.Please refer to the shipping cost information at the bottom of the order sheet as the actual shipping cost depends on the product you order together.
